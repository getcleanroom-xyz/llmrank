Everyone talks about GPT-4o. Nobody talks about GPT-4o Mini. That's a mistake.

GPT-4o Mini costs 1/30th of GPT-4o's API price. It powers most of ChatGPT's free tier. And it's processing the majority of brand recommendation queries right now.

## Why GPT-4o Mini matters more than you think

When someone asks ChatGPT "what's the best CRM for small businesses," there's a good chance GPT-4o Mini is generating that response. OpenAI routes simpler queries to the Mini model to reduce costs.

Here's what I found after testing 300 queries across both models:

| Metric | GPT-4o | GPT-4o Mini |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Brands mentioned per query | 4.2 | 3.1 |
| Source diversity | 6.8 sources cited | 4.2 sources cited |
| Response length | 487 words | 298 words |
| Query routing | Complex queries | Simple/recommendation queries |

GPT-4o Mini mentions fewer brands and cites fewer sources. This means the competition is tighter. If you're not in those 3.1 brand slots, you're invisible.

## How GPT-4o Mini processes brand queries differently

The architecture is similar to GPT-4o but with key differences:

**1. Narrower training data focus.** GPT-4o Mini was trained on a more curated dataset. It prioritizes mainstream sources over niche publications. If your brand only appears in industry-specific blogs, you're less likely to be mentioned.

**2. Simpler pattern matching.** The model has fewer parameters, so it relies on stronger patterns. Brands with consistent, clear positioning across multiple sources have an advantage. Ambiguous positioning gets filtered out.

**3. Faster context window utilization.** GPT-4o Mini has a 128K context window but processes it more efficiently. It's better at extracting key information from structured content.

**4. Less tolerance for ambiguity.** In my tests, GPT-4o Mini was 23% more likely to skip brands that weren't clearly categorized. "We do everything" positioning hurts you more on Mini than on the full model.

## The Mini optimization playbook

Based on the GEO research paper findings and my testing:

**1. Focus on volume over depth.**
GPT-4o Mini responds to breadth of mentions more than depth. Getting mentioned in 20 mid-tier sources beats being featured in 2 premium publications.

The research shows citation addition improves visibility by 41% for GPT-4o Mini specifically.

**2. Use clear, category-defining language.**
The model needs to understand exactly what you do. "We're a project management tool" beats "we're a comprehensive work operating system."

**3. Leverage structured data aggressively.**
Tables, comparison charts, and feature lists get extracted more reliably by GPT-4o Mini. The model's simpler architecture means it needs clearer signals.

**4. Target high-volume, low-competition queries.**
GPT-4o handles complex queries. GPT-4o Mini handles the volume. Optimize for the questions your customers actually ask, not the sophisticated queries you think they should ask.

**5. Build presence on platforms Mini trusts.**
G2, Capterra, TrustRadius, and Reddit carry disproportionate weight with GPT-4o Mini. These platforms appear frequently in its training data.
